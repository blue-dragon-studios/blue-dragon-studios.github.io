Donald Thomas

Donald Thomas has been making stuff since he was old enough to hold an object in each hand. From the English Language, to Paints, Leather, and even metal, Don's been trying to make cool stuff all his life. Don is the main artist at Blue Dragon Studios, and has experience making jewlery with meaning, and leather-works with purpose.

His lengthy beard has also earned Don the unofficial title of 'The Wizard of Hot Springs'. He is uncontested for the title.

Samuel Thomas

Samuel Thomas has been interested in computers since the times of bulky monitors and Macromedia Flash. Following in the footsteps of his nerd parents, Sam seeks to eventually create video games, instead of just playing them. Also acting as the webmaster at Blue Dragon Studios, he has almost no experience making jewlery, but can easily tell you about how Nintendo is historically better than everyone else in the video game industry.

Sam has no beard because he hasn't earned one yet. Writing his own 'bio' in third-person isn't helping him any either.
